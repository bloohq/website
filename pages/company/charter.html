This document reflects the philosophy we've developed since our founding in 2018, including extensive feedback from over 17,000 organizations across 120+ countries. As the future of work continues to evolve, our Charter will guide us in acting in the best interests of organizations worldwide.

To that end, we commit to the following principles:

Customer-First Independence

We commit to maintaining complete independence from investor pressures that might compromise our commitment to customer success, affordability, or long-term thinking. Our primary obligation is to our customers and their success.

As a self-funded company, we make decisions based on what customers actually need, not what maximizes quarterly returns or satisfies external stakeholders. Every feature, pricing decision, and strategic choice will be evaluated through the lens of customer benefit.

Our commitment: We will never compromise customer interests for investor returns.

Process Individualism

We reject the notion that all organizations should follow the same "best practices." True competitive advantage comes from doing things differently, not from copying what everyone else does.

While the industry pushes standardized workflows and generic templates, we believe organizations should differentiate themselves through their unique business processes. Your competitive edge shouldn't be sacrificed for the convenience of one-size-fits-all software.

Blue adapts to your unique processes, not the other way around. We provide the foundation for you to build processes that reflect your organization's unique strengths, culture, and strategic advantages.

Democratized Access

We commit to ensuring that powerful work organization tools remain accessible to all teams, not just well-funded enterprises. We will resist the industry trend toward increasingly complex and expensive solutions that exclude smaller organizations.

Enterprise-grade capabilities should not require enterprise budgets. We believe that a 5-person startup deserves the same sophisticated process management tools as a 5,000-person corporation.

Our commitment: We will aim to make a thoughtful pareto-princple trade off, providing 80% of enterprise functionality at 20% of the cost.

Technical Leadership

We commit to maintaining technical excellence at every level of our platform. We believe that superior engineering creates superior user experiences, and that cutting-edge technology should work invisibly to empower our customers.

While others chase trends or rely on outdated architectures, we invest deeply in performance, reliability, and security. We build our platform with the same rigor and craftsmanship that mission-critical systems demand, because your work deserves nothing less.

Our commitment: We will always choose the technically superior solution, even when it's harder to build.
